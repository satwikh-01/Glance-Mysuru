TOURISM
Each of these places in Mysore has its own distinct charm and contributes to the city's rich cultural and natural heritage.

Brindavan Gardens

Beautiful terraced gardens with symmetrical designs, musical fountains, and a popular spot for leisurely walks and picnics.

Chamundi Hills

Scenic hills with a revered temple dedicated to goddess Chamundeshwari, offering panoramic views of Mysore city.

Jaganmohan Palace

Former royal residence converted into an art gallery, housing a vast collection of paintings, sculptures, and artifacts.

Karanji Lake

Serene lake with a butterfly park and a museum showcasing the region's biodiversity and natural history.

Lalit Mahal

Luxurious heritage hotel known for its grand architecture and royal hospitality, offering a glimpse into Mysore's regal past.

Mysore Palace

Magnificent royal palace showcasing a blend of architectural styles and famous for its grandeur and illuminations.

Mysore Zoo

Well-maintained zoo housing diverse wildlife species, providing an opportunity for close encounters with animals and birds.

Ranganathittu Bird

Sanctuary

Picturesque bird sanctuary along the banks of the Kaveri River, home to a wide variety of migratory and resident bird species.

St. Philomena's Church

Impressive neo-Gothic church, one of Asia's tallest, featuring beautiful stained glass windows and a preserved crypt.

Mysore Rail Museum

It showcases the rich history of Indian railways through exhibits, vintage locomotives, and interactive displays.

A beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its intricate carvings and peaceful ambiance.

Nimishamba

TRADITIONS
Mysuru embraces its rich traditions through grand festivities like Dasara, exquisite silk sarees, intricate Mysore paintings, and age-old cultural practices that define its unique heritage.

Mysore Dasara

Ten-day festival with grand procession, cultural performances, and illumination of Mysore Palace; celebrates victory of good over evil.

Vairamudi Festival

Precious gems adorn the Vairamudi crown of Cheluvanarayana Swamy Temple in Melukote, attracting devotees.

Ugadi

New Year celebration with rituals, cultural performances, and sharing of Obbattu (Holige), symbolizing fresh beginnings.

Mysore Silk Sarees

Exquisite silk sarees known for intricate designs, vibrant colors, and gold zari work; symbol of cultural heritage.

Mysore Paintings

Intricate paintings depicting gods, goddesses, and mythological stories; characterized by natural colors, gold leaf work, and delicate brushstrokes.

Mysore Pak

Delicious sweet made of ghee, sugar, and gram flour; famous delicacy during festivals and special occasions.

Dasara Procession

Grand "Jumbo Savari" procession during Mysore Dasara with decorated elephants, cultural troupes, and floats.

Sandalwood Artifacts

Skilled artisans carve intricate designs on sandalwood, creating figurines, jewelry, and home decor; cherished souvenirs.

Mysore Peta

Traditional headdress, a turban-like cap worn by men; symbol of pride and honor.

Ilkal Sarees

Handwoven saree with distinctive red border and pallu; made with cotton and silk yarns, popular in Mysore and surroundings.

Glance Mysuru

Mysore is renowned for its pleasant climate, with moderate temperatures throughout the year, making it a delightful destination for tourists. The city is steeped in rich heritage, showcasing architectural marvels, ancient temples, and palaces that reflect its royal past. Mysore's vibrant culture, traditional arts and crafts, and delectable cuisine further enhance its charm, offering a captivating experience for visitors.
